The journal

Peer-reviewed. Open access. DOI-ready.

The IPA Journal publishes clinical research, protocols and case studies in point-of-care ultrasound twice a year, with an international editorial board drawn from the network's schools. The archive moves to an open-journal platform with ISSN registration and persistent DOIs for every article.
